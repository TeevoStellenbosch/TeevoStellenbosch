About TeeVo Club

TeeVo Club is a community where teenagers and young adults in Stellenbosch come together to explore God's Word in a practical and relatable way.

God's Word Made Practical

The group's name comes from the TeeVo devotional - which stands for "Teen Version" of God's Word. Everything we discuss comes straight from the Bible, helping you understand what Jesus came to do and the life God has made available for you.

In a world full of confusion and pressure, TeeVo Club provides a safe space to ask questions, build meaningful friendships, and discover your purpose through God's Word.

Whether you're new to faith or have been following Jesus for years, you'll find a welcoming community that will support and challenge you to grow spiritually.

Growing together in faith

What To Expect

At TeeVo Club, we've created an environment designed specifically for teenagers and young adults to engage with God's Word.

Biblical Teaching

Our discussions are based entirely on Scripture, making God's Word practical and applicable to your daily life as a young person.

Authentic Community

Connect with other teens and young adults who are navigating similar challenges and seeking to grow in their faith.

Relevant Discussions

We address real issues you face - relationships, identity, purpose, stress, and more - from a biblical perspective.

Safe Environment

Ask questions, share thoughts, and express doubts in a judgment-free space where everyone is respected.

Practical Application

We don't just learn information - we focus on how to live out biblical principles in school, relationships, and everyday decisions.

Fun & Connection

While we take God's Word seriously, we also enjoy hanging out, having fun, and building genuine friendships.
